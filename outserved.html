> Outserved Business

Outserved is an app which uses geofencing technology to assist businesses in determing important information about their user's interactions. They asked me to clean and model all of their data from the last 2 and 1/2 years. This project introduced me to the world of data science; I learned a lot about data cleaning and visualization, along with tools such as Numpy, Pandas, and Seaborn. This dataset did not provide enough data to train accurate models; however, I hope to start another project soon to learn more about machine learning and feature engineering.
